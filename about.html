Vijayawada Branch Profile

The Vijayawada Branch of Southern India Regional Council (SIRC) of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) was established on June 1, 1973. Over the last five decades, the branch has grown dynamically, responding to the growing needs of CA professionals and students in Vijayawada, Guntur, and surrounding parts of Krishna & NTR districts.

Our branch caters to more than 1,800 Chartered Accountants and over 8,000 CA students. The branch facilities include:

  • A fully equipped library with digital resource subscriptions.
  • Two modern air-conditioned reading rooms open 12 hours a day.
  • A seminar hall with 250+ seating capacity for hosting CPE conferences.
  • An integrated IT and computer training lab for AICITSS training.

Chairman's Message

CA. Prasad Rao K.V.S.

Chairman, Vijayawada Branch (Term 2026)

"Integrity, Professional Excellence, and Lifelong Learning"

Dear Colleagues,

It gives me immense pleasure to address you through our official web portal. As Chartered Accountants, we are the trustees of financial discipline and corporate governance. In this age of rapid digitalization, automation, and expanding tax laws, the role of a Chartered Accountant has transformed from a traditional auditor to a strategic business advisor.

Our branch committee is committed to providing world-class professional training. We are lining up intensive coaching programs for our students under the new scheme of education, and scheduling seminars on digital assets taxation, international audit standards, and real-time ledger auditing tools.

I welcome active participation from all members in our CPE circles and encourage students to actively participate in the SICASA forums.

Sincerely,
CA. Prasad Rao K.V.S.
